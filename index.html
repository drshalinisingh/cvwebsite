About Me

I am a passionate Assistant Professor of Anatomy and certified physiotherapist, committed to making medical education engaging, inclusive, and future-ready. My award-winning teaching harnesses cutting-edge digital methods, 3D visualization, and active mentoring to empower the next generation of doctors and healthcare leaders.

As a core content creator and curriculum designer, I develop competency-aligned e-learning modules, advise on national digital health projects, and bring a research-driven approach to every classroom. My interests span embryology, neuroanatomy, and innovative pedagogy—with a mission to bridge classic rigor with modern technology.
